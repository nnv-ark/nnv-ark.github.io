KLUKK! — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

KLUKK! is built and published by NNV ehf. In short: KLUKK! does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.

What we collect

Nothing. KLUKK! has no account system, no sign-up, and no servers of our own. We use no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs of any kind.

Data stored on your device

KLUKK! stores your timed sessions and your preferences locally on your device so the app works between launches. This information never leaves your device except in the ways you explicitly choose, described below.

The calendar you choose

When KLUKK! saves a session, it uses Apple's Calendar (EventKit) framework to create an event in the calendar on your device that you selected. Those events live in your own calendar, governed by your iCloud or device settings — not by us. We never see them.

Exports you initiate

If you export a session as a .ics file or your log as .xml, that file is created on your device and shared only if and where you choose, through the standard iOS share sheet.

Notifications

If you set a target time, KLUKK! schedules a local notification on your device so it can alert you when the time is reached. This happens entirely on-device and collects no data.

Tracking

KLUKK! does not track you across apps or websites and contains no tracking technologies.

Children

KLUKK! is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised policy at this URL.
