Bill To Book — Support

Scan a paper bill into a clean PDF and email it to the right inbox — in one tap.

Getting started

  • Add a company — set its sender details, a default recipient email, and the starting receipt number.
  • Tap the button and scan the document; pages are cropped and combined into a multi-page PDF automatically.
  • The PDF drops into a pre-addressed email, ready to send — override the recipient for a one-off whenever you need to.
  • Keep multiple companies, each with its own sender, recipient, and receipt counter.

Common questions

The email won't send. Bill To Book sends through your device's Mail account, so Mail must be set up in Settings → Mail. The PDF is attached to a normal outgoing message that you send yourself.

The scanner won't open. Grant Camera access in Settings → Bill To Book.

My receipt numbers are out of order. Each company has its own counter — check you're scanning under the right company, and adjust the next number in its settings.
