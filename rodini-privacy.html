RODINI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

RODINI is built and published by NNV ehf. In short: RODINI does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data. Everything happens on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. RODINI has no account system, no sign-up, and no servers of our own. We use no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs of any kind.

Camera & LiDAR

RODINI uses your device's camera and LiDAR scanner to scan objects and to place models in augmented reality. The camera feed and depth data are processed entirely on your device to build and display 3D models. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or seen by us.

Location

If you turn on real-sun lighting, RODINI reads your approximate location while you use the app for one purpose only: to compute the sun's true position in the sky so it can light your model to match. Your location is used on-device in the moment and is never stored, logged, or transmitted.

Microphone

The microphone is used only while you are recording a video, so that the video you capture includes sound. The recording is created on your device; nothing is sent anywhere.

Photos and videos you save

When you capture a photo or video of your AR scene, RODINI saves it to your Photo Library at your request. RODINI only adds the items you create — it does not read your existing photos.

Your models and exports

Models you scan or import are stored locally on your device. If you export a model (.usdz, .obj) or a raw capture, that file is created on your device and shared only if and where you choose, through the standard iOS share sheet.

Tracking

RODINI does not track you across apps or websites and contains no tracking technologies.

Children

RODINI is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised policy at this URL.
