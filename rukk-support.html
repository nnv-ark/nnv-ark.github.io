RUKK — Support

A fast, native macOS invoicing app for small Icelandic businesses and sole traders.

Getting started

  • Create a company — its name, kennitala, and bank claim details go on every invoice.
  • Add a customer, then create an invoice and fill in the line items.
  • Choose the invoice format in Settings → Invoice: the Icelandic layout (VSK per line, legal footer) or the simpler Universal English template.
  • Issue the invoice to lock its number, then export as PDF or e-invoice (UBL / TS-136 / PEPPOL), or email it straight from the app.
  • Import billable time from Tyme or Calendar as invoice lines from the Línur section of the editor.

Common questions

The app asks me to subscribe. RUKK requires an active subscription (“RUKK áskrift”, a yearly plan with a one-week free trial). Manage or cancel it anytime in the App Store → your account → Subscriptions.

I already paid but the app is locked. Open Settings and choose Restore Purchases while signed in to the same Apple Account you subscribed with.

Can I switch the language? Yes — set the interface and invoice languages separately (Icelandic / English) in Settings → Language.

Can I keep more than one company? Yes — each company keeps its own invoice numbering, customers, and invoices; switch between them at any time.
