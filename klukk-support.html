KLUKK! — Support

A one-tap stopwatch that saves each timed session to your iOS Calendar.

Getting started

  • On first launch, tap Link calendar and grant Calendar access.
  • Tap the big pink button to start; tap again to stop — the session is saved to your chosen calendar automatically.
  • Tap the time to set a target; KLUKK! alerts you when you reach it.
  • Add the Home Screen widget to start and stop without opening the app.
  • Export any session as .ics, or your whole log as .xml, from the calendar list and Settings.

Common questions

Nothing saves to my calendar. Make sure you granted Calendar access in Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars → KLUKK! (Full Access lets it also create calendars).

The target alarm didn't ring. Allow Notifications for KLUKK! in Settings → Notifications.
