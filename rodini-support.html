RODINI — Support

Scan real objects into 3D, then place them back into reality at true-to-life size.

Getting started

  • Scan — choose Scan an Object and walk slowly around it as the guide prompts you. Inspect the result on the turntable, name it, and keep it.
  • Place — pick a model, aim the reticle at a real surface, and tap to place it. Pinch, drag, and rotate to position; set its real-world size by height or scale.
  • Capture — switch to Capture mode to take a photo or record video of your AR scene.
  • Library — browse, import, and export your models. With iCloud on, your models sync privately across your own iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Common questions

Scanning isn't available on my device. Object Capture and real-world occlusion need a device with a LiDAR Scanner — iPhone 12 Pro and later, or an iPad Pro with LiDAR. Placing, importing, capturing, and exporting work on any supported device.

The camera view is dark or nothing appears. Grant Camera access in Settings → RODINI, and move the device slowly so it can find a surface.

My models aren't syncing. Make sure you're signed in to iCloud and that iCloud Drive is enabled for RODINI in Settings → your name → iCloud.

I need maximum detail. Export the raw capture and reconstruct it at full resolution on a Mac.
